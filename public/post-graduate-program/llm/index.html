Overview of LL.M

Master of Laws is a one year Post Graduate programme. The LL.M programme of ITM University is focused towards scholastic excellence in the realm of knowledge of Law. The course stimulates research and high proficiency in academics. The programme aims at creating the best of legal minds. The students are trained to develop an explorative and analytical mind to understand various laws. The employability quotient of the students is enhanced to meet the demands of both domestic and global job market. ITM University offers specializations in.

Business Laws Criminal law & Criminal Justice Administration

PROGRAMME FEATURES

  • Rigorous academic efforts in learning the laws; not limited to provisions but to develop analytical understanding of laws.
  • Practically studying laws i.e. to understand the laws in the perspective of its implementation and impact on the society.
  • Emphasis on précis research on various laws from the domain of specializations which enables to harness command over that specific law domain.
  • Exposure to serious academics including research publications, field projects and teaching assignments.

SPECIALISATIONS

BUSINESS LAWS

LL.M with Business Laws as specialization provides an in-depth knowledge of laws related to establishment of a corporate and the mandatory legal requirements of any business. The course provides scholarly training on various aspects of both domestic and international business laws. The course enables the scholar to understand the concept of various instruments; enabling and regulating both domestic and international business. It includes the laws relating to Corporate Finances, Mergers and Acquisitions, Takeovers, Intellectual Property, Corporate Social Responsibility etc. The Post-Graduate with specialization in Business Laws is empowered with ability to analytically study both global and domestic legal regulations related to corporate and provide competent consultancies.

CRIMINAL LAW & CRIMINAL JUSTICE ADMINISTRATION

LL.M with specializations in Criminal Law and Criminal Justice Administration provides a scholastic approach to the philosophy and concept related to the ‘Crime’ and its occurrence. This course begins with a detailed learning and understanding of the criminal jurisprudence in both the domestic and global connotations. The course includes a detailed study of the national laws regulating crime and criminal justice administration. Further, the course also includes an exposure to the international treaties and laws relating to crime. The course enables the student to develop the skill to study and critically examine various international mechanisms for criminal justice administration system and provide reasonable solutions. The specialization enables the post-graduate of law to analytically evaluate the contemporary criminal justice administration system of not only India but to compare it with the laws enforced around the globe. The scholar will be able to devise and propose the changes in the existing system so that it can be developed and evolved in such a manner so as to meet the expectations of all the stakeholders.
