Overview of Ph.D.

ITM University Ph.D. program is offered to the high profile individuals who are interested in making their career in industry and academics research. We offer this program to the students who completed their Post-Graduation in Management, Commerce, Law

The Ph.D. is offered in the streams in the:

Ph.D. in Management
Ph.D. in Commerce
Ph.D. in Law

Admission Process

The selection is through entrance test and interview by the expert panels. The minimum period of duration for earning PhD degree is three years and maximum period is six years. The student will have to undergo Course Work four subjects. Course Work will be delivered thorough the lectures by the competent resource persons in respective subjects. Upon completion of Course Work students will undergo the exam and successful students will be asked to submit proposal.
