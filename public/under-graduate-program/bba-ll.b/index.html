Overview of BBA LL.B Program

BBA.LL.B is an integrated 5 year undergraduate program, where simultaneous courses of Bachelor of Business Administration and Bachelor of Laws are administered. The program focuses on both the areas of knowledge related to Business Administration as well as Law.

The program includes rigorous training on the knowledge of fundamental laws, its interpretations and implementations. The teaching learning pedagogy includes meticulous research, experiential learning and analytical understanding of the subject through lectures, tutorials, case law methods, minor projects etc. The graduates are highly proficient with the nuances of business management and professionally skilled advocates of law.

FAQ of BBA LL.B

  • 1. What is a BBA LL.B program?

    Answer - A BBA LL.B program is an integrated dual-degree course that combines a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) with a Bachelor of Laws (LL.B) degree. It is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of both business and legal principles.

  • 2. What is the duration of the BBA LL.B program?

    Answer - The duration of the program is Five years.

  • 3. What are the eligibility criteria for admission?

    Answer - 10+2 Pass and/or as per the Bar Council of India norms.

  • 4. Are internships a part of the program?

    Answer - Yes, many BBA LL.B programs include internships or practical training components. This allows students to gain real-world experience in legal and business settings.

  • 5. What career opportunities are available after completing the program?

    Answer - Graduates can pursue careers as legal professionals, corporate lawyers, legal consultants, business analysts, compliance officers, or even start their own businesses. The dual degree provides a versatile skill set.

  • 6. Is there a requirement for a final thesis or project?

    Answer - The program may include a final-year thesis or project, where students apply their knowledge to a real-world issue or problem.

  • 7. Can I pursue higher education after completing BBA LL.B?

    Answer - Yes, after completing BBA LL.B, students can pursue postgraduate studies in law or business, such as an LL.M (Master of Laws) or an MBA (Master of Business Administration).
