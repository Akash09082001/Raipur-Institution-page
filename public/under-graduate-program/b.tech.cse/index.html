Overview of B. Tech.(Computer Science and Engineering)

The Computer Science and Engineering stream has evolved to a great extent during past two decades. New and promising technologies in computer science, such as artificial intelligence, high-performance computing, human-friendly intelligent software, next generation internet and communication, are becoming increasingly important throughout the industry. For this reason, the demand for advanced computer professionals in the industry is rapidly increasing. The programme has been designed in consultation with highly experienced academicians and industry experts of relevant field. This has made the curriculum of the programme exactly matching the needs of present and future IT and associated industries.

Highlights of the Program

The program focuses majorly on the following distinct propositions apart from availability of all basic programme requirements

  • Excellent coding environment under ‘ITM University Codechef Chapter’
  • Design and Programming skills development through Design Hackashop and Coding Laboratory
  • Product design & development through CAD/3D Printer
  • Industry specific employability skills development
  • Inclusion of certified Massive Open Online Courses (MOOC) in curriculum
  • Business communication skills development
  • Focused placement through Individual career plan (ICP) and capstone Project work

Future prospects of BBA (Scope)

After completing B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering, a students will have following career options

Web Developer / Software Developer
Mobile App. Developer
Game Developer
Data Scientist
Machine Learning Engineer
Full Stack Developer / DevOps Engineer
Multimedia Programmer
Network Security Engineer
Big Data Engineer
Cloud Engineer

In addition to these careers opportunities, the BBA graduates can pursue further education, such as a Master in Business Administration (MBA) or other postgraduate programs, to specialize in a particular area of business or management. They can also take up professional certifications, such as Certified Management Accountant (CMA), Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA), or Project Management Professional (PMP), to enhance their career prospects and salary potential.
